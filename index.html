Darren W. Jones

Two Trillion Galaxies is a 28-page crossover picture book/graphic novel at 660 words, written for ages 6–10. It turns astronomy’s biggest numbers into an intimate, wonder-filled journey. When eight-year-old Lucy hears scientists say there are two trillion galaxies in the observable universe, she can’t sit still. With her jetpack and helmet, she blasts off, scaling outward from Earth to the solar system, past the Milky Way, and toward the edge of everything.

DieselBrick City is a 260-word picture book for ages 4–7 that blends imagination, toy logic, and adventure as Captain Nate discovers amazing dimensional gates and must swap out specialized mechs to chase down the ninja-cat thief who stole his city's treasured Cruller Core. One donut. Magic dimensions. Infinite fun.

Love Lights the Way is a 290-word picture book for ages 5–8 that invites young readers on a journey from fear to safety, guided by a constant, comforting presence. For every child who’s ever felt afraid and every heart that helped them home.

When a child’s world shifts (a house left behind, a beloved pet gone, a grandparent’s passing) can anything still feel steady? When Things Change is a 153-word picture book for ages 4–6 that helps young readers face loss with gentle honesty and quiet hope. A story of love, change, and holding on to what matters most.
